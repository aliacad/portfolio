Planta

synopsis
The visual identity for this edible cookie company is designed to be fun, unique, and natural, emphasizing the 100% vegan nature of the cookies through the use of bright colors, hand-drawn illustrations, and typography. The logo features a serif typeface that has been altered to give it a sense of uniqueness and fun, making it stand out from other cookie brands.,
The color palette for the brand uses a combination of green, purple, and orange, which are natural, vibrant, and playful colors. The green represents the natural ingredients used in the cookies, while the purple and orange add a sense of fun and playfulness to the brand.
The hand-drawn illustrations used in the branding feature natural elements such as leaves, flowers, and fruits, reinforcing the natural and vegan aspects of the cookies. The illustrations are playful and whimsical, adding a sense of fun and personality to the brand.
